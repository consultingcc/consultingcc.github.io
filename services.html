Infant Care — A Safe and Nurturing Start

We provide infant care for ages 6 weeks to 2 years old. We have created a loving and calming environment for your baby to develop and be successful! Our infant nursery has been designed to provide warmth and security and to respond to your baby's intuitiveness.

Your primary caregiver is focused on the individual needs of your baby, and will work with you to mirror your existing routine with bottle times, naps and play times.

Core Curriculum — The Creative Curriculum for Infants

  • We incorporate music into your baby's day — songs like "Itsy Bitsy Spider" and "Wheels on the Bus" with hand motions.
  • Playing peek-a-boo with a blanket can send your baby into giggles.
  • We allow baby to play Follow the Leader — tapping, waving, and mirroring movements.
  • We play pretend using stuffed animals as members of the Nana's Little Elephants family for mind stimulation.

Our Philosophy

At Nana's Little Elephants, we believe every child deserves a nurturing start. Our curriculum is rooted in play-based learning, guided by qualified teachers who understand child development and celebrate every milestone.

Our Organization

We partner closely with families, offering regular progress updates, open-door communication, and parent workshops. When home and school work together, children thrive.

Partnerships with our team

We work closely with each family to understand their child's individual needs, interests, and learning style. Our teachers communicate openly with parents and collaborate as a team to ensure every child receives consistent, nurturing support throughout the day.
