INFANT CARE  

CARE SERVICES
 We provide infant care for  ages 6 weeks to 2 years old.  We have created a loving and calming environment for your baby to develop and be successful! Our infant nursery has been designed to provide warmth and security and to respond to your baby’s intuitiveness.
Your primary caregiver is focused on the individual needs of your baby, and will work with you to mirror your existing routine with bottle times, naps and play times.
CORE CURRICULUM -THE CREATIVE CURRICULUM FOR INFANTS
 

*  We incorporate music into your baby’s day.  We sing songs like “Itsy Bitsy Spider” and “Wheels on the Bus” with the hand motions.
* Playing peek-a-boo with a blanket can send your baby into giggles.
* We allow baby to  play follow the Leader.  See if she can tap after you tap, wave her hand after you do, and so on.
* We play pretend with you baby for mind stimulation.   We use stuff animals, as if other members of the  Nana's Little Elephants Center family. 

2 YEAR OLDS
CARE SERVICES
 Your toddler's growing curiosity, energy and enthusiasm with fun learning activities and creative playtime, while introducing structure and a routine is highly motivated here.  These experiences help make learning fun!
Your child's teacher will plan a day that includes story time, outdoor play, free play, song and dance.  We also work with you when your child is ready to start the potty training process.  We offer several creative techniques to help at home and at school.  

CORE CURRICULUM - THE CREATIVE CURRICULUM FOR TODDLERS & TWOS
 

* Costume trunks are used, for your child, to supports his interest of transportation and construction: police and firetruck outfits, plastic tools, etc.
* Children create  homemade instruments.  This includes saucepans, spoons, drums, bottles filled with rice, pasta or sugar, paper plates with metal curtain rings or bottle tops attached around the edge.
* Finger painting stimulates sensory development.  We use nontoxic paints and old newspaper or any paper for your child to use for his creations.
* Your toddlers love to paint, and we encourage them to.   We have paint time for the children to paint a picture for mommy and/or daddy.


(3 YEAR OLDS) TRANSITIONS
CARE SERVICES
 In our early preschool program, your active 2- or 3-year-old will participate in engaging experiences and environments that support the development of their brain, body and social-emotional skills.The educational childcare program is designed to provide the structure and support your child needs during this important transition from toddlerhood to preschool. 
CORE CURRICULUM - THE CREATIVE CURRICULUM FOR PRESCHOOL
 

* We encourage your child to dramatic play. Which means we have a stock of costumes and supplies for your preschooler to play pretend, cooking, building, etc.
*  We make use of natural materials.  We collect leaves, small rocks, and other things little things to use in art projects.
*  We have story time. Children pick out their favorite books for the teacher to read and act out the story.
* On a pretty day, children are taken outside.  They can have free play in the playground.
* We have  dance parties to the children's favorite songs.  
* We do  play-doh and slime play.  The children love to mold and cut out shapes!
* We incorporate limited screen time to get your child ready for the ever changing world of virtual learning.
