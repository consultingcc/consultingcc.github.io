About Our Financial Assistance Program

Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II accepts families participating in the subsidized childcare assistance program. If you think you may be eligible to receive childcare assistance, please apply for services through your participating state agency. If you are approved, you will receive a childcare voucher from your agency with instructions.
