(3 Year Olds) Transitions

Supporting your child's important transition from toddlerhood to preschool.

In our early preschool program, your active 2- or 3-year-old will participate in engaging experiences and environments that support the development of their brain, body, and social-emotional skills. The educational childcare program is designed to provide the structure and support your child needs during this important transition from toddlerhood to preschool.

Core Curriculum — The Creative Curriculum for Preschool

  • Dramatic play with a stock of costumes and supplies for cooking, building, and pretend play.
  • Nature art projects using collected leaves, small rocks, and other natural materials.
  • Story time where children pick their favorite books for the teacher to read and act out.
  • Outdoor free play in the playground on nice days.
  • Dance parties to the children's favorite songs.
  • Play-doh and slime play — molding and cutting out shapes.
  • Limited screen time to prepare children for virtual learning.

Frequently asked questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Monday through Friday from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and all major holidays.

What does tuition cost?

We offer competitive, affordable pricing for all families. A discount is available for second children enrolled at the center. Financial assistance options are also available for qualifying families — please contact us to learn more.

How do I enroll my child?

You can get started by submitting an enrollment inquiry on our Contact page. A member of our team will follow up within 1 business day to walk you through the enrollment process and answer any questions.

What is your drop-off and pick-up policy?

Drop-off begins at 6:00 AM and pick-up must be completed by 5:00 PM. For safety, only authorized adults listed on your child’s enrollment form will be permitted to pick up. Please bring a valid photo ID at pick-up.

Our benefits
  • Licensed and experienced educators dedicated to each child's growth.
  • Safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment every day.
  • Open communication with parents and guardians.
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your family's needs.
  • Financial assistance options available for qualifying families.
