Welcome

Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II

Our program is designed to promote the social, physical, and cognitive development of every child. Our goal at Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II is to help each and every child feel good about themselves by building their self-esteem!

The love, understanding, and care your child receives from Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II will also help reinforce positive self-image. Every child is treated with respect and dignity. In doing so we will teach the children to behave and respond in a positive manner. All our facilitators are required to take continuing education classes through DSS every year. We hope you enjoy our site — take a moment to drop us a line and schedule a center visit today!

Our Features

Our program is grounded in The Creative Curriculum and built around the needs of infants, toddlers and preschoolers. Every activity — from music and art to outdoor play and story time — is designed to nurture curiosity, confidence and joy.

Safe Environment

Our facility is designed with your child's safety first — secure entry, age-appropriate spaces, and trained staff who maintain a clean, nurturing environment every day.

Educational Activities

Every day is filled with intentional, play-based learning that sparks curiosity, builds foundational skills, and makes education joyful for young minds.

Qualified Teachers

Our dedicated educators are trained in early childhood development and committed to building meaningful, caring relationships with every child.

Infant Care

We provide attentive care for infants 6 weeks to 2 years old, working closely with parents to mirror home routines and support healthy early development.
