    Welcome

    Our program is designed to promote the social, physical, and cognitive development of every child. Our goal at Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II is to help each and every child feel good about themselves by building their self-esteem!

    The love, understanding, and care your child receives from Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center/ Phase II will also help reinforce positive self-image. Every child is treated with respect and dignity. In doing so we will teach the children to behave and respond in a positive manner. All our facilitators are required to take continuing education classes through DSS every year. We hope you enjoy our site — take a moment to drop us a line and schedule a center visit today!

    Meet our Talented Team

    The heart of Nana's Little Elephants is our dedicated, highly qualified team. Every teacher holds credentials in early childhood education, completes ongoing professional development, and is CPR and First Aid certified. Together, we create a warm community where every child feels known, loved, and ready to learn.
