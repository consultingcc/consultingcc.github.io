2 Years Old

Building curiosity, confidence, and social skills at every step

Your toddler's growing curiosity, energy and enthusiasm are met with fun learning activities and creative playtime, while introducing structure and routine. Your child's teacher will plan a day that includes story time, outdoor play, free play, song and dance. We also work with you when your child is ready to start the potty training process, offering several creative techniques to help at home and at school.

Core Curriculum — The Creative Curriculum for Toddlers & Twos

  • Costume trunks with police and firetruck outfits, plastic tools, and more to support your child's interest in transportation and construction.
  • Children create homemade instruments — saucepans, drums, bottles filled with rice or pasta, and more.
  • Finger painting with nontoxic paints to stimulate sensory development.
  • Dedicated paint time so children can create a picture for mommy and/or daddy.

Infant Care

We provide infant care for ages 6 weeks to 2 years old. We have created a loving and calming environment for your baby to develop and be successful! Our infant nursery has been designed to provide warmth and security and to respond to your baby's intuitiveness.

  • Your primary caregiver mirrors your existing routine with bottle times, naps and play times
  • We incorporate music into your baby's day with songs like "Itsy Bitsy Spider" and "Wheels on the Bus"
  • Creative activities including peek-a-boo, follow the leader and pretend play for mind stimulation

2 year olds

Your toddler's growing curiosity, energy and enthusiasm with fun learning activities and creative playtime, while introducing structure and a routine is highly motivated here. These experiences help make learning fun!

  • Story time, outdoor play, free play, song and dance every day
  • Support for the potty training process with creative techniques at home and at school
  • Hands-on activities including finger painting and homemade instruments

3 year old (transitions)

In our early preschool program, your active 2- or 3-year-old will participate in engaging experiences and environments that support the development of their brain, body and social-emotional skills.

  • Dramatic play with costumes and supplies, story time, and outdoor playground time
  • Dance parties, play-doh and slime play for creative and sensory development
  • Limited screen time to prepare children for the ever-changing world of virtual learning

First Steps 4K Program

Our Pre-K program prepares your child for success in Kindergarten and beyond.

Core Curriculum — The Creative Curriculum for Preschool

  • We encourage your child to act out his favorite books during story time.
  • We encourage your child's artistic side — creating a classroom gallery with one painting framed each week.
  • Pretend time for your child to explore the world indoors and outdoors.
  • We prepare your child to be socially and mentally ready for the transition to Kindergarten.

Afterschool

We provide transportation from your child's local school to our facility once school is dismissed for the day. We provide a safe, supervised, and fun environment for children ages 5–12 to end the school day.

Core Curriculum — The Creative Curriculum for Family Care and Distance Learning

  • Teachers on-site to help with homework assignments and school projects.
  • Virtual learning available upon parent request.
  • A safe, nurturing environment to transition from the school day.
