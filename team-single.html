Shirley Tucker

Owner & Founder

Shirley founded Nana's Little Elephants CDC with a simple belief: every child deserves to feel loved, safe, and inspired.

About Shirley

Shirley Tucker is the founder and owner of Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center, located in Columbia, South Carolina. With decades of experience in early childhood education and an unwavering commitment to the children and families she serves, Shirley built NLE from the ground up on the principles of love, safety, and intentional learning.

Shirley's journey into early childhood care began long before the center opened its doors. As a mother, grandmother, and community advocate, she understood firsthand how transformative high-quality childcare could be — and how difficult it was for families in Columbia to find it. She set out to create something different: a place that felt like an extension of home, staffed by educators who genuinely cared about each child as an individual.

Today, Shirley continues to be a daily presence at the center, working alongside her team and staying closely connected to the families who trust NLE with their most precious ones. Her vision shapes everything from curriculum choices to how families are greeted at the door.

Leadership & Qualifications
  • Founder and Owner, Nana's Little Elephants Child Development Center
  • Licensed by the South Carolina Department of Social Services (DSS)
  • Extensive background in early childhood education and child development
  • CPR and First Aid Certified
  • Active community advocate for quality childcare access in the Midlands
  • Dedicated to ongoing staff training and professional development
